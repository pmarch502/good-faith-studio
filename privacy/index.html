Privacy Policy

The short version: we collect nothing.

This policy applies to all apps published by Good Faith Studio, including Mizar: Star Battle Puzzle.

What we collect

Nothing. Our apps do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There are no accounts and no sign-up.

Tracking and ads

None. We do not track you, we show no ads, and we include no analytics or third-party advertising or tracking software of any kind.

Data on your device

Your settings and puzzle progress are saved locally on your device so the app remembers where you left off. This information never leaves your device and is never sent to us or anyone else. Removing the app removes it.

Network

Our apps work fully offline and make no network connections to us.

Children

Because we collect no data at all, our apps are safe for players of any age. We never knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, we will update this page and revise the date below.
